Anatomy of a Piece

Secondhand Fashion Archive

In this archive, we showcase a diverse collection of secondhand items, each with its own unique history and significance. These pieces have been carefully gathered from members of Generation Z, who have shared not only the pictures of the items but also their personal stories and experiences attached to them. Through this collection, we aim to highlight the style and emotional value of pre-loved fashion and accessories, emphasizing sustainability, individuality, and the evolving relationship between younger generations and secondhand clothing.
